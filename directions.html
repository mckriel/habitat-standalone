DIRECTIONS

From Ceres, follow the R46 East in the direction of Touws River. 11km past Ceres turn right onto the Bo-Swaarmoed road. Follow this road for 19km (up the pass and past the Klondyke Cherry Farm)

Don't turn right until you see a sign that says Matroosberg, 2km. Follow the dirt road to the gate straight ahead. Please drive slow on the dirt road and in the reserve

Driving time and distance: From Cape Town - 2 hours, 164km (via Bainskloof)
